## From Seven Bridges to Six Degrees

Euler demonstrated: a path that traverses each edge of a network only once must depend on the degree of the nodes (the number of edges touching each node). Graph must have either two nodes with an odd number of edges or none.

**Degree centrality**: The number of edges radiating out from a specific node and captures sociability. 
**Betweennness centrality**: extent to which information passes through a particular node. 
**Closeness centrality**: Average number of steps it takes for the a node to reach all other nodes and is often used to discover who has the best access to information. #metric
