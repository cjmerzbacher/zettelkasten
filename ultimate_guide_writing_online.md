# The Ultimate Guide to Writing Online

## The Age of Leverage
People can access your knowledge without taking your time if you write for them.

## Create an Online Home
Start Here page
Curated list of your favorite articles
To build your website without sacrificing quality, curate: (1) Thought leaders’ ideas (2) Curate list of best resources on a topic
Focus on oen platform and stick to it (email)
Focus on quantity over quality first
**Consistency develops ability**

## The Five Pillars of Writing
1. Write evergreen content
2. Publish simple, quality content
3. Be specific
4. Listen to feedback and iterate through ideas before publishing
5. Repackage your existing work
