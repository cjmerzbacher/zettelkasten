# System Resilience: What Exactly is it?
Donald Firesmith, Software Engineering Institute

Two applications of systems resilience: data centers, cloud computing, cyber-physical systems.
Other quality attributes: availability, reliability, robustness, safety, security, and survivability - how does resilience connect to these?
Disruptions are inevitable. Resilience must be broken down:
* protects critical capabilities from disruption caused by adverse events and conditions
* Avoidance is not resilience - it decreases the need for resilience.

Protection:
* Resistence: ability to passively prevent/minimize harm from occurring during adverse event or condition (modular architecture)
* Detection: detection of loss/degradation of critical capabilities, harms etc.
* Reaction is active
* Recovery
