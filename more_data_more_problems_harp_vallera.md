Consent is something given by someone, to someone, for something. 

Good for:
* respect for autonomy
* legal permissability 
    * Distiinguish legal, moral permissibility, moral good/bad
* forfeiture of right to complain

Problems with consent in data privacy
* Your data doesn’t just contain information about you: distributed control over identifying information

Instead, revise our idea of what data practices ought to be allowed vs. ought not to be allowed. This revises the problem from an individual one to a collective one.
