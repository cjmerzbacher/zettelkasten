### How to get shit done
2 reasons you’re not getting things done: (1) you don’t have a system (2) you aren’t managing your thoughts.
System: a consistent set of practices to manage your workflow
LIE: you don’t have time to make a system. it is a good investment.
Elements:
1. standard procedure for assessing how to get things done.
    2. Multiple inboxes (email, phone, class) to a single inbox (app, planner)
3. Go through and process inbox at designated times
    4. 2 minute rule - DO IT NOW
    5. Add to ongoing project list
    6. Additional task on existing project
7. Once a week, assign this week’s goals, projects, to time on your calendar
    8. Include buffer time
    9. Include rest, workouts, etc.

### How to stop settling
Settling is an unconscious choice to go with the stream - comfortable. 
ACTUALLY like a kind of death. No growth, no change.
You are hiding from the life you want. There is nothing wrong with the person you’re settling for, you are just harboring contempt for them and insulting them. 
We settle because of fear and scarcity thinking
	We don’t believe we have control and that our only other option is nothing.
    If I knew i could succeed in X, would I do this?
 Can masquerade as maturity or gratitude. You can appreciate the now and work to change
 
 (1) What does the dream look like?
 (2) What steps would you have to take?
 (3) What reasons can you NOT do it?
 These are just thoughts.
 (4) Make a “baby step thought” to stay instead of the final thought.

### Numbing and Compulsive Behavior
Judgement comes from ranking others to raise ourselves up. We’re only preoccupied with ranking others when we’re afriad they’re better than us. We must resolve our shame and self-judgement FIRST. Anything you’re doing to numb from your life’s emotional experience:
Why are you doing it
How do you feel before, during, and after doing it
Do you feel anxious about not being able to do it

Feelings are harmless if we just allow them and process them
Numbing consciously for a period of time is OK! Just something humans do that we can choose to do more or less of. Practice paying attention to yourself and plan for set times of behavior. Allow the urges to pass/
