Steps for modeling system: 
1. Define boundaries 
2. Observe agents
3. Observe stocks in system
4. Identify ecosystem services as flows
5. Include	 production functions for these goods/services
6. Define value and price
    7. Note this is not always well defined by the market (I would argue often the metaphor of the market is wrong). 

Methods: 
Damage cost avoided
Behavior averted
Adjusted market prices
Revealed/stated preference

Lower-experience, non use goods are problematic
Spatially explicit value functions
Marginal benefit curves
