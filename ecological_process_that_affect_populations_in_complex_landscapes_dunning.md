The composition of habitat types in a landscape and the…spatial arrangement of those habitats are the two essential features that are required to describe any landscape. 

4 ecological processes that can influence population dynamics or community structure:
1. Landscape complementation: multiple types of patches needed, movement between patches
2. Landscape supplementation: movement to supplement too-small patches to neighboring ones
3. Source-sink dynamics (different individuals in same population occupying different quality habitat patches)
4. neighborhood effect: amplifies based on critical resources in immediate surroundings

Landscape scale: between organism’s home range and regional distribution
