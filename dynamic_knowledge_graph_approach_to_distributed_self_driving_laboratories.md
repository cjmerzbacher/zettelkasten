- Affiliations: Cambridge, Singapore
- World Avatar project: seeks to create a digital twin based on a dynamic knowledge graph
- Two labs, one in Singapore and one in Cambridge, linked by shared ontology (dynamic knowledge graph)
	- Abstracts software components as agents that receive inputs and produce outputs, physical entities virtualized as twins
	- Actions generate changes to the knowledge graph
- GoalSet -> Goal -> Plan -> Steps as DAGs
- Application: finding pareto front between cost and yield for manufacturing conditions of chemicals
"Notably, the Singapore setup encountered a...failure...resulted in a wrongly identified peak..." - Singapore lab went offline for technical reasons, Cambridge continued.
- Ontologies "an iterative process and not a goal in and of itself." - expert, human-derived ontology - this is quite manual! 
- "Overall, the ontology development process starts as easily as drawing concepts and their relationships on a whiteboard and then gradually materialising them in code."
